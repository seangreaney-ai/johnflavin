# Project Context — Wood Interiors by John Flavin

## The Business

**Wood Interiors by John Flavin** is a custom fitted furniture maker run by John Flavin, based in **Duagh, Co. Kerry, Ireland**. John designs and installs bespoke furniture for homes across Kerry and Munster. Every piece is made to measure — kitchens, wardrobes, living room units, utility rooms, bathroom furniture, and wall panels.

John works directly with customers: he visits the home, takes measurements, designs the solution, and installs it himself. There is no middleman. Hardware comes from trusted suppliers — primarily **Häfele** and **Hendel & Hendel** for handles, and **Blum** for soft-close hinges and drawer systems.

## Brand Voice

- Straightforward and honest — no marketing fluff
- Craftsman-led: quality of work speaks first, personality second
- Warm and local — written for Kerry homeowners, not a national chain
- Descriptive and specific about materials (colour names, finish types, hardware brands)
- Copy leans on detail: the Blanco Stellar quartz, the Knurled Gold knobs, the Raw Endgrain Oak interior

## Target Audience

Homeowners in Kerry and Munster planning a renovation or new-build fit-out. They care about quality and finish, want something personalised, and are unlikely to buy from a flat-pack retailer. They may have seen John's work through word-of-mouth or local referrals.
