The Craftsman

About John Flavin

John Flavin has been designing and installing custom fitted furniture across Kerry and Munster for over a decade. Based in Duagh, Co. Kerry, John works with homeowners to create furniture that fits their home perfectly — in every sense of the word.

Every project starts with a conversation. John visits your home, listens to what you need, and designs a solution that works for your space and your lifestyle. Whether it's a full kitchen fit-out, a run of bedroom wardrobes, or a bespoke TV and display unit for your sitting room, the same care and attention goes into every job.

"Good furniture isn't just about how it looks — it's about how it works for the people who live with it every day."

John works with trusted suppliers to source the best materials — from painted smooth-finish doors and solid oak to luxury quartz worktops and quality Häfele hardware. Every piece of furniture is built and installed by John himself, so you always know who's responsible for your project.

John serves customers across Kerry and Munster, including Tralee, Listowel, Abbeyfeale, Limerick, and the surrounding areas. Free home consultations are available — get in touch to arrange yours.
