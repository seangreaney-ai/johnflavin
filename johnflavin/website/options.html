Worktops

Worktop Materials

We supply and fit a range of worktop materials to suit every style and budget — from practical laminates to luxury quartz and natural stone.

Quartz

Durable, low-maintenance, and beautiful. We work with several quartz suppliers including Elm Fireplaces and Full House. Popular colours include Blanco Stellar, Treaty White, and Carrara.

Solid Oak

Warm, natural, and timeless. Solid oak worktops are a popular choice for traditional kitchens and utility rooms. Available in several stain and lacquer finishes.

Laminate

A practical, cost-effective option available in a huge range of colours and patterns — including marble effects, timber effects, and solid colours.
