Bathroom Furniture

Vanity units, linen presses, and fitted bathroom storage — water-resistant finishes available, made to measure for any layout.

Bathroom furniture from John Flavin is built to the same standard as everything else — made to measure, properly fitted, and finished to last. Whether you need a vanity unit to fit around your basin, a full linen press, or storage to suit an awkward space, John designs it to fit perfectly.

Vanity Units

Built around your basin and plumbing — with drawers and doors to maximise storage under the sink.

Linen Presses

Tall storage units for towels, bedding and toiletries — fitted to the full height of your bathroom wall.

Water-Resistant Finishes

Bathroom-grade board and moisture-resistant door finishes available for all units in wet areas.
