06 — Services

Wall Panels

Classic and contemporary panel styles for hallways, bedrooms and sitting rooms — available in any painted colour.

Wall panelling adds character, texture and depth to any room — and John installs it to the same high standard as all his fitted furniture. From traditional raised-and-fielded panels to sleek contemporary slat walls, each installation is made to measure and painted in your chosen colour.

Classic Panelling

Raised-and-fielded or shaker-style panels — a timeless choice for hallways, dining rooms and bedrooms.

Slat Walls

Contemporary vertical or horizontal slat panelling — popular in sitting rooms and bedrooms as a feature wall.

Any Painted Colour

Panels are painted in any colour — Farrow & Ball, Colortrend, or any standard paint range to match your interior.
